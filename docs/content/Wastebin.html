Wastebin

Write and fail
Write and fail
Crumble the paper
Into the wastebin
to die
Try try try
To achieve that light
That light beams
perfection
or so you think
The light often
illuminates parts
That you did not
see
did not think of
before
Maybe all those
attempts
Made the beam
brighter
